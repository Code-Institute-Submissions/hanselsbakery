Customer Service

Ordering & Deliveries

For orders please use our contact form on our website.

We have a limited area of deliveries. If you wish to place an order, please get in contact via our contact form or calling at 020 252 5689.

Click and Collect

We offer click and collect, you can place your order by calling us on 020 252 5689 or by submitting an enquiry via our contact form.

Cancellations

We only accept cancellations if it is within 5 hours of placing an order, otherwise we would not be able to guarantee that your order would be cancelled.

Changes to existing orders

The time limit to change your order is 5 hours from the time of placing it. If it is passed the time limit we cannot guarantee changes to your order.

Refunds and Returns

If order was cancelled within the time limit we will be ale to give full refund. All returns are only possible if there was an allergic reaction, however make sure you get in contact to know diatery and allergens before placing an order.
