Product Information

Freshly Baked Cakes

Our cakes are freshly baked always using high quality ingridients to achieve the best flavours for our products.

Storage and Shelf Life

The storage of our products is between 0 and 5 degrees celcius to maintain optimum shelf life. We keep our products in storage for a maximum of 3 days.

Dietary & Allergens

Our products may contain nuts, gluten, diary, eggs. Please, talk to one of our staff or send us an email from our contact page to know more about it.

Some of our products are available in vegan version at request.
