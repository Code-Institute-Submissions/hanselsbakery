Our Story

Our story started when I was hired for first job at a local bakery, and when I graduated from Culinary School, I was promoted from retail sales to cake decorator. From this point onwards it was when I first realised I loved what I was doing, the feeling of creating something that will impact someone’s life.

After 10 years experience in the industry I decided to open my own bakery shop in 2019, after hiring a team of pastry chefs, I have personally mentor them to produce unrivalled cakes that would be the most popular in London. Since then, we have grown from 5 chefs to now 10 and we continue to thrive depiste the climate of COVID-19.
